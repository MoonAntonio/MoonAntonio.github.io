Rob Paulsen

Actor | Soundtrack | Director
Rob Paulsen was born on March 11, 1956 in Detroit, Michigan, USA as Robert Fredrick Paulsen III. He is an actor, known for Animaniacs (1993), Pinky y Cerebro (1995) and Las Tortugas Ninja (2012).
