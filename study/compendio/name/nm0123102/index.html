Hedy Burress

Actress | Soundtrack
Official Photos »
Hedy Burress was born on October 3, 1973 in Edwardsville, Illinois, USA as Heather Elizabeth Burress. She is an actress, known for Fainaru fantajî X (2001), Qué les pasa a los hombres (2009) and Fainaru fantajî X-2 (2003). She was previously married to Gary Robert Fullerton. See full bio »

Did You Know?

Trivia:

Her husband, Marine Corps Reservist, Major Gary Robert Fullerton, was a pilot on an F-18 Hornet attached to VMFA-134 during a training flight. His aircraft collided with another F-18 aircraft on July 21, 2004 during training near Arlington, Oregon. Both crew members were killed. He is buried at the Ft. Rosecrans National Cemetery in San Diego, California. See more »
