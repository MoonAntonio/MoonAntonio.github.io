Dwight Schultz

Actor | Soundtrack | Miscellaneous Crew
Dwight Schultz was born on November 24, 1947 in Baltimore, Maryland, USA as William Dwight Schultz. He is an actor, known for Star Trek: Primer contacto (1996), El equipo A (2010) and Vampire hunter D: Bloodlust (2000). He has been married to Wendy Fulton since June 12, 1983. They have one child.
