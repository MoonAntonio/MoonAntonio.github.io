Simon Templeman

Actor | Soundtrack
Official Photos »
Simon Templeman was born in Sussex, England. He is an actor, known for Legacy of Kain: Defiance (2003), Dragon Age: Origins (2009) and Blood Omen: Legacy of Kain (1996). He has been married to Rosalind Chao since 1989. They have two children. See full bio »
