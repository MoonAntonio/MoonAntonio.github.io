Grant James (I)

Actor | Writer | Miscellaneous Crew
Grant James was born on June 19, 1935 in Medina, Ohio, USA as James M. Grant. He is an actor and writer, known for Tombstone: La leyenda de Wyatt Earp (1993), Dos tontos todavía más tontos (2014) and Bernie (2011). He has been married to Juli Erickson since July 4, 1986. See full bio »
