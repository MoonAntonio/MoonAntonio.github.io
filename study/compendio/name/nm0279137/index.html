Takayo Fischer

Actress
Takayo Fischer was born on November 25, 1932 in Hardwick, California, USA as Takayo Tsubouchi. She is an actress, known for Piratas del Caribe: En el fin del mundo (2007), Moneyball: Rompiendo las reglas (2011) and En busca de la felicidad (2006). She has been married to Seymour Fischer since November 29, 1980. They have three children. See full bio »
