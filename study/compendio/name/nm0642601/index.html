Colleen O'Shaughnessey

Actress | Miscellaneous Crew | Soundtrack
Colleen O'Shaughnessey was born on September 15, 1971 in Grand Rapids, Michigan, USA as Colleen Ann O'Shaughnessey. She is known for her work on Danny Phantom (2004), Digimon Adventure tri. Saikai (2015) and Sonic Boom (2014). She has been married to Jason Villard since November 11, 2000. They have two children. See full bio »
