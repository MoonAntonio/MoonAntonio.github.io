Bernice Stegers

Actress
Bernice Stegers was born on July 12, 1949 in Liverpool, England as Bernice Mary Stegers. She is an actress, known for Cuatro bodas y un funeral (1994), Fainaru fantajî XII (2006) and Grandes esperanzas (2012). She is married to Mike Newell. They have three children. See full bio »
