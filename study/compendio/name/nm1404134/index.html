Brian Jepson

Actor
Brian Jepson was born on August 27, 1970 in Houston, Texas, USA as Brian Thomas Jepson. He is an actor, known for Kagaku ninja tai Gatchaman (1978), DC Universe Online (2011) and Metroid Prime 3: Corruption (2007). He has been married to Courtney Fowles since August 11, 2007. See full bio »
