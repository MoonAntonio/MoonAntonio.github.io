Julia Fletcher (I)

Actress
Julia Fletcher was born on November 2, 1956 as Julia K. Fletcher. She is an actress, known for Vampire hunter D: Bloodlust (2000), Fainaru fantajî X (2001) and Final fantasy: La fuerza interior (2001). She has been married to John DeMita since February 3, 1992. They have two children. See full bio »
