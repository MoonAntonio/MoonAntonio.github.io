Jamieson Price

Actor | Stunts
Jamieson Price was born on April 28, 1961 in West Palm Beach, Florida, USA as Jamieson Kent Price. He is known for his work on Akira (1988), Final Fantasy VII - Advent Children (2005) and El patriota (2000). He has been married to Bethany Price since December 31, 1993. They have three children.
