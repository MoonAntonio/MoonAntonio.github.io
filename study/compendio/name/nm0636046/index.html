Nolan North

Actor | Miscellaneous Crew | Soundtrack

Nolan North was born on October 31, 1970 in New Haven, Connecticut, USA as Nolan Ramsay North. He is known for his work on Uncharted: The Nathan Drake Collection (2015), Con Man (2015) and Pequeñas mentirosas (2010). He has been married to Jill Murray since June 4, 1999. They have two children. See full bio »
