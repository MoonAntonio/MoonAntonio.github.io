Grant George

Actor | Casting Department | Miscellaneous Crew
View Resume | Official Photos »
Grant George was born on May 19, 1971 in Watsonville, California, USA as Grant Raymond George. He is known for his work on Pulse (Conexión) (2006), Mysterious Skin: Oscura inocencia (2004) and Fate/Zero (2011). He has been married to Jessica Gee-George since September 24, 2003. They have two children. See full bio »
