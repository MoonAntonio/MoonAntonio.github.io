Dave Fouquette

Actor | Special Effects | Miscellaneous Crew
Dave Fouquette was born on February 21, 1964 in Tempe, Arizona, USA as David Brian Fouquette. He is known for his work on Metal Gear Solid V: The Phantom Pain (2015), Troops (1997) and Ghost Rider (2007). He is married to Ann Marie Solar-Fouquette. See full bio »
