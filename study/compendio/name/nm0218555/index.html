John DeMita

Actor | Miscellaneous Crew | Sound Department
John DeMita was born on January 6, 1959 in New York City, New York, USA as John J. DeMita. He is known for his work on Vampire hunter D: Bloodlust (2000), La princesa Mononoke (1997) and Black Mask (1996). He has been married to Julia Fletcher since February 3, 1992. They have two children. See full bio »
