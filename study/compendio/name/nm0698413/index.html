Phil Proctor (I)

Actor | Miscellaneous Crew | Writer
Phil Proctor was born on July 28, 1940 in Goshen, Indiana, USA as Philip George Proctor. He is known for his work on Monstruos, S.A. (2001), Assassin's Creed (2007) and Borat (2006). He has been married to Melinda Peterson since 1992. He was previously married to Barbro Semmingsen. See full bio »
