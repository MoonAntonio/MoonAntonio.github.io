Soldier

Appearances: FF12RW, FF Tactics, FFTA
Aliases: Sky Pirate, Sky Soldier, Knight, Warrior, Raptor

The Soldier's job is usually to break or reduce an enemy's fighting capability, either by destroying its equipment or its stats. A break-off class from Warrior and Knight.
Similar Job Classes: Knight, Warrior
