NAME:Arithmetician/Calculator (PSX)
OVERVIEW: This class is known as one of the more effective in battle, due to its ability to affect many units without charge time or MP cost.
WEAPONS: Stick, Dictionary
ABILITIES/MAGIC:
Arithmeticks/Math Skill (PSX):
  • CT - Math skill to calculate CT
  • Level - Math skill to calculate Level
  • Exp - Math skill to calculate Exp
  • Height - Math skill to calculate height
  • Prime/Prime Number (PSX) - Math skill that guides selected item in a random prime number 1-100
  • Multiple of 5/ 5 (PSX) - Math skill that guides selected item in multiples of 5
  • Multiple of 4/ 4 (PSX) - Math skill that guides selected item in multiples of 4
  • Multiple of 3/ 3 (PSX) - Math skill that guides selected item in multiples of 3
    Reaction Abilities: Cup of Life/Distribute (PSX) - share excess HP with allies when HP is maxed, Soulbind/Damage Split (PSX) - shift damage received to enemy
    Support Abilities: EXP Boost/Gained Exp Up (PSX)
    Move Abilities: Accrue EXP/Move-Get Exp (PSX),Accrue JP/Move-Get JP (PSX)
    • ATTRIBUTES: Low evasion, low speed
