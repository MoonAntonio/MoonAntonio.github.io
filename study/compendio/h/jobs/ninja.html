Ninja

Appearances: FF1, FF3, FF4, FF5, FF6, FF7, FF11, FF12RW, FFT, FFTA, FFTA2
Aliases: None

Ninjas are usually associated with the Throw command which allows the user to take an item or unequipped weapon from the inventory and throw it at the enemy for massive damage. The downfall is this item/equipment is lost forever as soon as it is thrown, so it's essential to choose wisely. They also come with high speed, high agility, and an affinity for thievery. They also have the staple of being able to equip a sword in each hand, doubling their attack power.

Similar Job Classes: Thief, Samurai, Knight

ATTRIBUTES: Average strength, high agility, high luck, low magic
MAGIC:
Black Magic: Fire, Lock, Slep, Lit, Dark, Slow, Tmpr, Ice, Fir2, Lok2, Hold, Lit2, Conf, Fast, Slp2, Ice2

FF3
NAME: Ninja
OVERVIEW: This class is best physical strength job class (as opposed to the Shaman whose primary power is magic). It does not share many characteristics with the other Ninjas.
WEAPONS: All weapons, but primarily shuriken
ABILITIES/MAGIC:
  • Fight - Attack with equipped weapon
  • Defend - Increase defense power for one turn
  • Run - Run away from battle
  • Item - Use an item from inventory
    • ATTRIBUTES: High strength, high HP, low magic defense, low magic

    WEAPONS: Uses blades best

  • Sneak - Steal an item from the enemy

  • Flame - Cause fire damage to all enemies

  • Blitz - Cause damage to all enemies
  • Pin - Paralyze one enemy
  • Image - Project two images of the self to avoid the next two physical attacks

    FF5

    NAME: Ninja
    OVERVIEW: This Ninja also uses the Double Lance which can strike twice.
    WEAPONS: Knives, Full Moon, Rising Sun, Double Lance
    ABILTIIES/MAGIC:
  • !Smoke - Escape from battle quickly
