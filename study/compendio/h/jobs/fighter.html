Fighter

Appearances: FF1, FF2, FF3, FFT, FFTA, FFTA2
Aliases: Squire, Soldier, Warrior, Mog Knight, Moogle Knight

Not to be confused with Warrior, which was originally known as Fighter, the contemporary fighters usually have access to a variety of skills with which to cause heavy damage to enemies. On the flip side, they often have lower defense and straight-up strength than Knights or their spinoffs. Similar Job Classes: Knight
