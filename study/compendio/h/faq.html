In the video game world, FAQ means more than just "Frequently Asked Questions". It refers to a document which details as much information as possible about the game it's on. This usually (for RPGs) includes a walkthrough and a list of items, spells, shops, etc. If you want easy-to-download FAQs, I suggest looking in GameFAQs. Basically all the information you need can be found in the FAQs and maps linked below. The FAQs have been checked by our staff to verify that they are as complete as possible, but if for some reason, you still get stuck, contact information for the author(s) should be noted somewhere in the FAQ. If that gets you nowhere, you can try me as well.
