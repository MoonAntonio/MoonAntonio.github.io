Final Fantasy 1 Characters

We're missing some screenshots from the PSP version. If you have an overworld PSP screenshot of any of the characters below; especially Chaos, Garland, Kraken, Lich, or Marilith; please send them here!

Arylon
A dancer in Cornelia. She's only given a name in the NA NES version.

Expand Images

Astos
The Dark Elf who steals Matoya's Crystal to be able to see, and puts the Elf Prince to sleep.

Expand Images

Bahamut
The king of dragons gives the honor of a class change if you bring him a Rat's Tail. (See also Summon:Bahamut)

Expand Images

Bikke
A pirate who terrorizes the port town of Pravoca, and who gives you his ship when you beat him.

Expand Images

Chancellor
(DoS only) Description forthcoming.

Chaos [VILLAIN]
The ultimate evil, a big guy with wings. Incidentally, one of Vincent's Limit Breaks in 7 allows him to turn into Chaos. Kind of cheap, isn't it?

Expand Images

Cid
(DoS only) Description forthcoming.

Darryl
Though she never mentions her name when you speak to her, it can be deduced through dialogue with a mermaid in the sunken shrine that she was a former mermaid who grew legs. She comments on how nice her legs are. Only named in NA NES version.

Expand Images

Garland [VILLAIN]
A knight who forges an unholy alliance with the Four Fiends, entering a 2000-year loop which renders him practically immortal. He transforms into Chaos, the ultimate evil (and will knock you all down).

Expand Images

Jane
(Aliases: Jane (NES) Jayne (PSX, DoS)) The Queen of Cornelia, Sarah's mother. She worries about her daughter.

Expand Images

Jim
(Aliases: Jim (NES), Watts (DoS)) A dwarf who's investigating the overworld.

Expand Images

Kary
(Aliases: Kary (NES NA), Marilith (NES JP, PSX, GBA) The Fiend of Fire. Note that she actually resembles Kali, the multi-armed Hindu deity; the first documented instance of Engrish! Kary reappears in FF9 as the Chaos of Fire, Maliris.

Expand Images

Maliris from FF9
Kope
(Aliases: Kope (NES), Koppe (PSX, DoS)) A man in Onlak who saw a robot fall to earth.

Expand Images

Kraken
The Water Fiend, who resides in the Sunken Shrine. He comes back in FF9 as the Chaos of Water.

Expand Images

Kraken from FF9
Lich
The Earth Fiend holes up in the Terra Cavern just waiting for some Heroes to munch. He comes back in FF9 as the Chaos of Earth.

Expand Images

Lich from FF9
Lukahn
(Aliases: Lukahn (NES, DoS), Lukin (PSX)) The head of the Circle of Sages was the one who prophecied the coming of the Light Warriors.

Expand Images

Marilith
See Kary.

Matoya
A witch who keeps special herbs and needs a crystal ball to see.

Expand Images

Nerrick
(Aliases: Nerrick (NES, DoS), Nelic (PSX)) The dwarf builder who makes a canal with TNT.

Expand Images

Sara, Princess
(Aliases: Sara (NES), Sarah (PSX, DoS)) Quintessential plot element, captured by evil knight Garland. (See also Character:FF3)

Expand Images

Sarda
(Aliases: Sarda (NES), Sadda (PSX, DoS)) A sage who gives you a rod to break a plate blocking a staircase in Lich's cave.

Expand Images

Smith
(Aliases: Smith (NES), Smyth (PSX, DoS)) The dwarf blacksmith who makes Xcalibur if you give him Adamant. See Kokkol from 4.

Expand Images

Thule
(DoS Only) Description forthcoming.

Tiamat
The Wind Fiend, who lives in the Flying Fortress atop the Tower of Mirage. He reappears in FF9 as the Chaos of Wind.

Tiamat from FF9
Titan
(Aliases: Titan (NES), Giant (DoS)) A squarish, blue-skinned guy who eats rubies. See Summons for more info.

Expand Images

Underhill
The caravan master who bottles fairies for a living and sells them for ridiculous prices.

Unne, Dr.
(Aliases: Scholar Une (JP)) A professor who's studying Leifinish.
