Adsense - Western Union sender's name from Google Asia Pacific

Just some quick info here. Previously I took my Adsense payments by using Google Inc. as sender name without any problem, until Adsense has been upgraded to new payments system, the payment displays Google Asia Pacific as the payer (Asia-Pacific region). If you are under this category, you have to fill Google Ireland Ltd. as your sender in Western Union agent.

Adsense - Youtube payment for April 2013 and onward

Earning from Youtube show up on my Adsense on 15th of May, 2013


Many users asking about the new payment formula after Google made some Analytics changes, caused Youtube's earning won't show up in Adsense as well as the finalized monthly payment. I share my experience for my first payment after the changes hope to help anyone still confusing with this.

This month - May 2013 supposedly I should get my payment from Youtube and Adsense ads, but it only shows me the Adsense earning. From the info I found from many websites, Youtube earning would only apply around 10th - 15th of the month, so after a 2 weeks waiting time, the second row of the April's earning finally appear which is the finalized earning for Youtube! Check the image above.

So here's the new formula after the changes:
- Youtube earning of the month will apply on 10th - 15th in the following month.
- To view Youtube earning in your Adsense, you need to go "Performance reports" tab, click the "Products" on the left column, click "Hosted AdSense for Content". The "Total Earning" listed your Youtube earning.
