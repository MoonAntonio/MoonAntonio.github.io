Maps

Click a location to see a collection of maps for that area. Items and switches are indicated by numbers, and capital letters connect one map to another. Walls or obstacles colored yellow (or another strange color) indicates secret passages that can be walked through. Some locations are simple enough to not require maps, so they are not provided here.
